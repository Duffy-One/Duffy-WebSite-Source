Bernard Duffy

This page is an Homage to Bernard Duffy, the "Father" of the Duffy Group.

He was born and grew up in and around Aghabog, Co. Monaghan, Ireland.

He began life as a Manual Labourer, and then trained to be a Psychiatric Nurse, at St. Patrick's Hospital in Dublin.

He later became a Milk-Man and, later still, a Security Guard.

He was a sensitive yet strong, under-stated man.

Bernard Duffy (Rest In Peace)
